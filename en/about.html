About

We believe in clear architecture, where function guides form and materials express their nature. The studio coordinates craftspeople and suppliers, caring for proportions, light and tactile quality.

Giulia Longhi develops residential, commercial and renovation projects with an essential, contemporary approach.

Philosophy. We design with a few essential elements: natural light, proportion and authentic materials. Every project seeks durability, clarity of use and balance between function, detail and atmosphere.
