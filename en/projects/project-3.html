Medical Practice GC

Location
Genova, Italy
Site
70 m²
Materials
Plaster, mouldings, colour
Status
Completed

A medical practice and waiting room are reimagined as places of quiet welcome: recomposed mouldings define discreet backdrops, with tactile surfaces and desaturated colours. Layered lighting, both technical and atmospheric, orchestrates visual comfort while ensuring privacy, calm and understated contemporary elegance.
