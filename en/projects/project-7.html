Flower shop

Location
Genova, Italy
Site
retail - 18 m²
Materials
Wood, glass
Status
Completed

The restoration of a historic flower kiosk in Castelletto, Genova, enhances its original character while improving comfort. The roof was insulated for thermal and acoustic performance, while the interior was fully restyled with light finishes, diffuse lighting and modular IKEA furniture, selected and adapted to optimise space and function. A new side opening creates a threshold between inside and outside that becomes, in summer, a teaching area for floral workshops, supporting natural ventilation, visual continuity and a more direct relationship with the neighbourhood.
