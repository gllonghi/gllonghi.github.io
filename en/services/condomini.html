Services

Condominiums

Technical support to address condominium issues through an orderly path, from the initial assessment to the management of the intervention.

We support administrators and owners in reading the problem, coordinating the parties and defining clear, feasible technical solutions.

Start the preliminary check-up

Preliminary assessment of the issue

  • Analysis of the reported critical issue and the condominium context.
  • Technical site visit to verify existing conditions.
  • Discussion with the administrator to clarify the technical and operational framework.
  • Identification of possible intervention paths and the most suitable strategy.

Technical mediation and coordination

  • Support for dialogue between the parties to reduce conflict and misunderstandings.
  • Technical, management and condominium framing of the issue.
  • Coordination with complementary professionals when needed.
  • Guidance toward a shared, clear and feasible solution.

Technical management of the issue

  • Operational handling of the matter through to the definition of the required intervention.
  • Detailed site visits and targeted technical checks.
  • Specifications, quantity survey and support in reviewing estimates.
  • Site supervision, safety and management of technical and bureaucratic procedures.

Technical consultancy for disputes

  • Preparation of expert reports and specialist technical documentation.
  • Technical assistance for notices, preventive technical assessments and party-appointed consultancy.
  • Support to the appointed lawyer in defining the technical aspects of the dispute.
  • Preparation of useful technical elements for starting and structuring the procedure.
