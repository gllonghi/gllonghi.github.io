Services

Investors

Strategic analysis and enhancement of real estate operations, from the initial reading of the property to the definition of the most effective intervention.

We support clients in transforming an existing asset into a concrete opportunity, with attention to potential, positioning and the quality of the final result.

Start the preliminary evaluation

Preliminary assessment of the operation

  • Initial analysis of the property and its transformation potential.
  • Technical site visit with a critical reading of strengths, limits and opportunities.
  • First strategic vision of the intervention in relation to final value enhancement.
  • Identification of the main technical, layout and administrative issues.

Enhancement strategy

  • Structuring the operation in relation to the market, positioning and expected result.
  • Definition of the intervention level most consistent with the property profile.
  • Creation of a design vision capable of increasing attractiveness and perceived value.
  • Balance between intervention quality, final image and overall feasibility.

Intervention design

  • Technical and administrative feasibility review.
  • Development of the transformation project and redistribution of spaces.
  • Definition of aesthetic concept, materials and finishes.
  • Design drawings and technical indications for operational development.

Operational management and realization

  • Preparation of specifications and precise definition of works.
  • Selection and coordination of the companies involved.
  • Supervision of work and control of correct execution.
  • Site supervision and management of technical procedures connected to the intervention.
