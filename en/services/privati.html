Services

Private clients

A complete service for people buying, renovating or transforming a home, from the first evaluation of the property to the definition of the interiors.

We support private clients through every phase of the project with a clear, measured and concrete process.

Understand whether it is the right home
Estimate your renovation budget

Pre-purchase site visit

  • Analysis of purchase needs and objectives.
  • Property visit with a first general technical assessment.
  • Quick indicative survey and review of critical issues.
  • Preliminary layout hypothesis and initial cost estimate.

Design

  • Metric and systems survey of the property.
  • Administrative feasibility review, constraints and permits.
  • Aesthetic concept with palette, mood board and design drawings.
  • Initial cost framework and preliminary review of tax incentives.

Site preparation

  • Final and executive design with construction details.
  • Definition of materials, finishes, specifications and schedule.
  • Coordination of contractors, craftspeople and technical consultants.
  • Site supervision, cost control and permit management.

Complete realization

  • Definition of the identity of each room and interior design.
  • Custom furniture design and lighting design.
  • Coordinated selection of finishes, sanitaryware, windows and furniture.
  • Integration of existing furniture, move planning and final placement.
